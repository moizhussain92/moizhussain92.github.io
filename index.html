About ME

Hi, I am Moiz Hussain, a Software Engineer at MICROSOFT CORPORATION, Redmond, WA.

I am passionate about automation and you will constantly find me automating tasks that otherwise require significant amount of manual effort. I enjoy scripting, designing, front-end development and always look for opportunities to learn and implement the best practices at work. My main areas of interest include Azure, .NET MVC, Python and PowerShell. During my leisure time, I love playing Cricket and visiting the Mountains!

EXPERIENCE

I have worked at Polycom Inc. as a grad Intern for 3 months on technologies like Elastic Search, Amazon S3 and CentOS. My work involved developing scripts and tools to analyze Polycom call logs and represent the patterns visually.

Joined Microsoft in 2016 as a college hire. I worked in Enterprise Commerce division as a Service Engineer for the first 1.5 years and mainly focused on monitoring, telemetry, troubleshooting and managing incidents. Since the last 6 months, I have been working as a Software Engineer in the same organization with revised focus on bug fixes and automation. Over the course of my educational curriculum and professional carrier, I have developed core skills in C#, SQL, Networking, Troubleshooting, PowerShell, Python and Linux.
